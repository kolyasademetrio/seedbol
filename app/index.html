Since its founding, SEED eatery, now Seedbol has been called the best restaurant in Rehoboth Beach!

The Food

GLUTEN-FREE ∙ DAIRY-FREE ∙ EGG-FREE

We begin our day with the highest quality ingredients, preparing everything we serve from scratch each and every day. We prioritize sourcing from local farms to ensure the freshest and highest quality produce for our dishes. Our commitment to using all-natural ingredients means that you can enjoy our food with confidence, knowing that it is free from artificial additives and preservatives.

When you visit Seedbol, you'll find a variety of delicious food options available in our cafeteria-style line. From our cold and hot side stations to our selection of healthy vegan treats, there's something to satisfy every craving. We also take pride in offering seasonal kombucha that is made in-house, providing a refreshing and flavorful beverage option.

It's no surprise that SEED eatery, now Seedbol, has been nominated for the best restaurant in Rehoboth Beach. We are dedicated to providing a dining experience that not only delights your taste buds but also supports your well-being.

Join us at Seedbol and discover why we are the go-to destination for those seeking delicious, wholesome, and accommodating food. We can't wait to serve you under our new name and continue our commitment to excellence.
